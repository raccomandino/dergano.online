About Me

Dr. Francesco Dergano, born in 1992 in Italy, is a renowned specialist in business and big data technologies. He achieved remarkable success at a young age, becoming one of the youngest name partners at Bitcoin System Engineering Departments and Sky Crypto BV. Later, he joined Structural Integrity Associates as a senior executive team member.

Dr. Dergano's career path led him to the prestigious SINOPEC superlarge petrochemical global industry, where he played a crucial role as CEO in rescuing the company from bankruptcy in the early 2019s. He then took on the role of CEO and investment trust at DHL Airways and spearheaded various UK political projects. Currently, he serves as the Chairman and CEO of Flash Crypto Finance Government Protocol, Inc.

In addition to his impressive professional achievements, Dr. Dergano founded Skydatasol Ltd in 2023, liquidating his investments with a net asset value of £100 billion. Under Skydatasol Ltd, he initiated two projects: Project KamiWeb and Project KamiEngineering. The timeline for the completion of these businesses and their franchising opportunities is currently unknown. Dr. Dergano mentioned that he is occupied with University courses and Jurisdiction Jobs, and he does not plan to step away from his commitments until after 2026.

Project KamiWeb focuses on web application development, mobile apps, desktop apps, and website solutions, Marketing and Press-Room while Project KamiEngineering likely specializes in engineering-related endeavors. Dr. Dergano's dedication to his ongoing responsibilities suggests that the fruition of these projects and franchising opportunities may take some time or find a combination having the rights employees.

SkyDataSol is a Business Startup that is seeking investments to integrates the following goods and services:

1. Petrochemical Extraction and Refinement: SkyDataSol operates in the petrochemical industry, employing advanced technologies for the extraction and refinement of petroleum-based resources. The company emphasizes sustainable practices, focusing on minimizing environmental impact, reducing emissions, and optimizing resource efficiency.

2. Franchising, Data Centre and Software Development: SkyDataSol offers franchising opportunities to entrepreneurs interested in entering the petrochemical sector. It provides comprehensive support, including software solutions tailored to the industry's specific needs. These software applications assist with inventory management, logistics, production planning, and other operational aspects.

3. Solar Panels and Renewables for Domestic and Aerospace: SkyDataSol extends its reach into the renewable energy sector by specializing in the production and installation of solar panels. It aims to make clean energy accessible to both domestic and aerospace applications. SkyDataSol develops solar solutions for residential, commercial, and industrial purposes, as well as designs and implements solar power systems for aerospace applications, such as satellites and space stations.

4. Civil Constructions and Engineering: SkyDataSol has a dedicated division for civil construction and engineering services. This division undertakes infrastructure projects, ranging from roads and bridges to buildings and urban development. The company's emphasis is on sustainable construction practices, incorporating energy-efficient designs, green materials, and innovative engineering solutions to minimize environmental impact.

By combining these diverse goods and services, SkyDataSol creates a unique business model that integrates traditional industries with sustainable practices, technology-driven solutions, and a focus on renewable energy. It strives to be at the forefront of the petrochemical, software development, renewables, and civil construction sectors, while contributing to a greener and more sustainable future.
